Welcome to DECAF2

A Markovian Dependability Model with Cascading Failures

Mihir Sanghavi and Sashank Tadepalli, undergraduate students at New Jersey Institute of Technology alongside Professor Marvin Nakayamam, have developed a cutting-edge implementation of DECAF.

Note:  The "Reused Trees" statistic is slightly different between the two
       versions of code.   We try to represent this statistic as the count of
       times a tree was NOT calculated, but the tree was picked up from cache.
       As noted by the results, more trees were hits from cache in the new code
       - this is primarily due to the power of the BreadthHistory logic developed
       by Mihir.
